What Do You Do? Scenario Practice

Scenario 1: You receive a phone call from someone claiming to be your bank.

They say your account has unusual activity and ask for your login details.

  • A) Give them your login details immediately.
  • B) Hang up and call your bank using the official number.
  • C) Write down your details and think about it later.

Scenario 2: You get an email with a link claiming you won a prize.

  • A) Click the link to claim your prize.
  • B) Delete the email and report it as spam.
  • C) Reply to the email asking for more info.

Scenario 3: A caller claims to be your grandchild in trouble and asks for $1000 urgently.

  • A) Send the money right away.
  • B) Hang up and call your grandchild to verify.
  • C) Ask the caller to send proof and then decide.
