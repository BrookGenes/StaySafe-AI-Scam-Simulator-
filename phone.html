Phone Call Scams Learn To Spot AI Voices

“Your Grandson Needs Help!”

Real Voice:

AI‑Cloned Scam Voice:

How to Find the AI Voice:
  • The voice may sound too smooth or slightly robotic.
  • Emotion changes may sound “flat.”
  • There may be odd pauses or repeated tone patterns.
  • Scammer pushes urgency (“Send money now!”).
How To Respond Safely:
  • Hang up immediately.
  • Call your actual family member directly using a known number.
  • Never send money or gift cards from a phone call.

“Bank Fraud Alert”

Real Bank Representative:

AI‑Generated Fake Bank Caller:

Warning Signs:
  • Real banks do NOT ask for PINs or passwords on calls.
  • AI voice may sound “too perfect” or emotionless.
  • Caller ID can be faked don’t trust it.
Correct Response:
  • Hang up and call the number on the back of your bank card.
  • Do NOT give any personal information.

“Police Department Warning”

Real Police Officer:

AI‑Cloned Fake Officer:

Key Red Flags:
  • Police NEVER demand payment by phone.
  • AI voice may mispronounce your name strangely.
  • Scammer threatens arrest or legal trouble a common tactic.
Safe Response:
  • Hang up immediately.
  • Call your local police station using the official number.
  • Report the scam attempt.
